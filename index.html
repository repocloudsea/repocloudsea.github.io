What is CloudSea

CloudSea is a technological startup aiming to make its mark in the cloud arena. It promises to provide SaaS, PaaS, and IaaS based solutions for businesses ranging across verticals. Its an organization having a vision and farsightedness of a large enterprise combined with the leanness and agility of a cool startup. It is determined to work towards creating softwares, platforms and infrastructure in cloud, thus enabling businesses to run smoothly and hassle-free.

What is inside our heart

Of all the clouds in the sky, this cloud dreams of having a silver lining

  • Vision

    Technology and innovations are the latest natural resource in the world, and CloudSea believes that every industry, big or small, should have easy access to it. Thus lies its mission of enabling every business, a small organization or any large enterprise, with the power of technology. It aims to provide solutions for every business need by managing complexities, logistics, downtime and overall risk of failure. There lies a large number of companies which are still running in an unstructured way, limiting its own growth and hindering its success. Few cannot afford the cost and others are reluctant to manage the technical overhead. We come with a mission of structuring them by managing all their storage, workflow, and complexities by moving them to cloud

  • Mission

    CloudSea has set its vision and is focussed to become the most dependable, affordable, and easily manageable cloud service provider in business. The organization not only limits itself to providing business solutions but even aligns itself to improve and empower the open source community. It wants to be a one stop solution , a guide and helper to millions of developers and engineers around the globe by providing them with platforms and infrastructures to build their applications on. The Cloud Folks, i.e the team of CloudSea, encourages the use of opensource in building solutions thus reducing cost, mantaining transparency and getting issues fixed by the ever growing community of contributors.
